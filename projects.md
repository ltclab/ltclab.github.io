# Funded Projects

Our research is made possible by the generous support of federal grants, corporate research partnerships, and foundational endowments. 

---

## Active Grants

### 1. Robust NLP for Low-Resource Languages
*   **Sponsor:** National Science Foundation (NSF)
*   **Focus:** Developing sample-efficient machine learning models that can achieve high contextual accuracy using limited training corpora.

### 2. Cultural Contextualization in Machine Translation
*   **Sponsor:** National Endowment for the Humanities (NEH)
*   **Focus:** Building translation models that identify and preserve cultural idioms, historical nuance, and localized dialects during text transformation.
